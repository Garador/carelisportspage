The Disney Experience

Offered by Disney®

DISNEY’S MAGICAL EXPRESS®

If you are flying into Orlando International Airport, you may take advantage of Disney’s Magical Express®, a complimentary service that will transport you, your team and your team’s luggage from Orlando International Airport to your Resort.

WALT DISNEY WORLD® THEME PARK TICKETS

Step off the field and into the magic of Walt Disney World® Resort. When you compete at ESPN Wide World of Sports Complex, your sports travel experience doesn’t end when the last whistle blows. Experience the world-class Walt Disney World® Resort Theme Parks or cool off in the wave pools and water-coasters at the two amazing Water Parks.

ESPN WIDE WORLD OF SPORTS COMPLEX

ESPN Wide World of Sports Complex can host more than 60 sports across ten world-class venues designed to give every athlete the best possible competition experience. It’s the pre-eminent multi-sport facility of its kind in the world.
